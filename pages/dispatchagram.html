📷 dispatchaGRAM

🙇‍♀️ Problem:

With such a high volume of orders being sent out, it was becoming harder and harder to go back and review photos that dispatch had taken if there was ever an issue with a job. Previously all the photos taken in a day were just dumped into one folder with no quick way of finding all the photos related to one job.

🙌 Solution:

I created a simple web application that was accessible by tablets on the shop floor to take photos and then anybody in the office could review the photos taken via a web browser.

💻 Screenshots:

home page (where the user can can enter a new job or search for a previous job in history)

history page (showing all the jobs, date and time they were entered and the no. of photos attached)

job page (you can see all the images attached to a job or add more images to the job)

reviewing a photo (lightbox gallery opens when you click on one of the photos)
